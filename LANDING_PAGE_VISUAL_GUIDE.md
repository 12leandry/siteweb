## Interactive Features

### 1. **Services Grid**
- **Hover**: Card scales 105%, shadow increases, icon rotates
- **Scroll**: Cards fade in and slide up with staggered delays
- **Click**: Navigate to service detail page

### 2. **Distinguishes Us Cards**
- **Click**: Selected card shows gradient background
- **Scale**: Selected card scales up
- **Color**: Card text turns white on selection

### 3. **Portfolio Carousel**
- **Left/Right Arrows**: Navigate between projects
- **Dot Indicators**: Click to jump to specific project
- **Counter**: Shows current project (1/4)
- **Auto-update**: Content changes smoothly

### 4. **Testimonials**
- **Auto-scroll**: Left column scrolls up, right scrolls down
- **Infinite Loop**: Testimonials repeat seamlessly
- **Hover**: Cards scale and shadow increases

### 5. **Header Navigation**
- **Sticky**: Navbar stays fixed at top
- **Scroll-aware**: Background changes on scroll
- **Language**: FR/EN switcher
- **Theme**: Light/Dark toggle
- **Mobile**: Hamburger menu on small screens
